The Outlands Series

The Outlands series is a trilogy that's yet to be completed. The Outlands series main theme is mentall illness. In recent times mental illness has become a major issue affecting especially the youth.

The series is centered around two siblings, seperated at a young age and each developing in two contrasting environments. Leroy depicts the worst of the outlands while Wendy's role shows wha society will do to keep itself alive and prosperous, with a little exaggeration, of course.

The books in the series in order are

  1. The Outlands
  2. The Insane
  3. Phantoms
